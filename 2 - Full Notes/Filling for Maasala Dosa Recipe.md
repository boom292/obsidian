# Filling for Maasala Dosa Recipe

## Ingredients

No exact measurements, if you've never eaten this before you'll probably mess it up. But for a seasoned (experienced) eater of this food this should be easy to eye ball.

Let's call the below category A. (You'll see why later)
- jeera
- rai
- heeng
- red chilli (depends on you)
- sesame oil (this a lot)

Lets call the below category B.
- curry leaves (I personally like very few, but you do you)
- onions
- haldi
- green chilli

- Mashed boiled potatoes (preferably peeled (after boiling)).

## Cooking

*Step 1:* Season category A on a hot pan

*Step 2:* assorté the above in the same pan and let the flavor spread across all of 'em.

Step 3: Add mashed boiled potatoes in there along with the above. Give it good a mix, this is the hardest step of them all.
